OVERVIEW
You will help us develop a community around the software framework for the design and discovery of new advanced materials and chemicals (think J.A.R.V.I.S from the well-known movie).

Work will focus on (1) identifying strategic pathways for community growth, (2) preparing the relevant content including case studies and technical presentation, (3) delivering content and measuring KPI.

Successful candidates will continue into a leadership role as we grow.

This is a full-time permanent position.
